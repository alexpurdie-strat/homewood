About Homewood
Rooted in faith, driven by compassion
For more than 90 years, Homewood Living Ministries has honored Christ through faithful service to seniors.
Our Founding
Where we came from and where we are today
Founded in 1932, Homewood began as a small faith-based community in Williamsport, MD. What started as a single location has grown into a network of five communities across Maryland and Pennsylvania.
Today, Homewood serves hundreds of residents guided by the same foundational values that have carried the organization for over nine decades. Growth has always been measured by quality of care.

A Life Plan Community provides multiple levels of care — from independent living to skilled nursing — all in one place. You move through different levels of care without leaving the community you call home.

Home›Residential Living
Residential Living
Find Your Place at Plum Creek
Retirement living the way it should be — maintenance-free, community-rich, and supported every step of the way.
Our Philosophy
A sense of home and true independence
Residential Living at Plum Creek offers the freedom to enjoy retirement without the stress of daily house maintenance. No more mowing the lawn in the summer heat, or bundling up to shovel snow — you can sit back, relax, and spend your time doing the things you love.
With a wide variety of activities, neighbors who become friends, and a beautiful campus to explore, every day brings something new. And when your needs change, you're already in the right place — with priority access to the Sandra L. Fair Healthcare Center, your future is always well supported.
If you ever need a little extra help, our nursing staff can assist you right in your home. The freedom is yours — live retirement at its best.

About Us
A community where you are family
Homewood Living Plum Creek has been serving the Hanover community since 1957, on over 100 beautifully developed acres.
Our Community
Purpose-filled, joyful retirement living
We believe in leading purpose-filled and joyful lives at every age. Plum Creek provides a variety of services, learning opportunities, and engaging experiences to enrich daily life. We are a Christian ministry — one of five communities operated by Homewood Living Ministries — and we welcome everyone regardless of faith or background.
